We Are

Undwear Because We Care is a non-profit organization in the Quad Cities that collects basic necessities and delievers them to those who are in need. The last campaign(which happend in 2015) collected 9200 items, helping over 2700 indiviuals. To checkout more about our history Click Here.

The Mission

The Mission of UBWC is to provide the basics necessities to every single houshold inside of the Quad Cities. The passion and drive behind what we do is making sure every child, mother and father has the things that they need in order to live a happy life.

Donating

100% of all donations go straight to purchasing new underwear, socks, toothpaste and much more. Your donations impact famlies all across the Quad Cities.
