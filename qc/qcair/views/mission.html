Mission of Quad Cities Alliance for Immigrants & Refugees:

We Give 100% Period.

Quad Cities Alliance for Immigrants & Refugees (QCAIR) is a not-for-profit organization that helps local immigrants and refugees with everything from acquiring citizenship to finding a job. QCAIR was founded in 2012 to help refugees and immigrants with issues they face every day as they try to adjust to an unfamiliar culture.

Our Mission: We assist immigrants and refugees in integrating into the Quad City community by advocating for immigrants and refugees and collaborating with appropriate service providers.

Our Vision: Every immigrant and refugee will integrate in the Quad City community successfully.

Our Core Values: Quality, Collaboration, Integration, Advocacy, Respect

Quality

Choosing the best people and procedures for the growth and sustainability of our organization and the people we serve. Always having people centered culture.

Collaboration

Collaborating with local and non-local agencies and donors to improve the lives of immigrants and refugees in the Quad Cities.

Integration

Educating immigrants and refugees on how to blend both cultures by exposing them to new ideas and experiences in their new home without letting go of their own beliefs.

Advocacy

Being a voice for immigrants and refugees in order to navigate and overcome social and cultural barriers. Believing in every person, desiring to see their best come to fruition.

Respect

Honoring every individual we come in contact with regardless of background, ethnicity, culture, nationality, status, color or belief. In this way we promote unity and diversity.
